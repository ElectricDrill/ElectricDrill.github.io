SOAP RPG Framework

A simple and flexible RPG framework for Unity. With respect to other more complex frameworks, it aims to provide a more accessible and simpler solution for developers and game designers. It is also designed with low assumptions, to impose minimal constraints on the user.

SOAP RPG Health

A health and damage framework for RPGs. It is developed with the same principles as the SOAP RPG Framework, and it is thought to integrate seamlessly with it. If you want to speed up your RPG development, this is the perfect add-on to manage health, damage, resistances, healing, death and respawn mechanics.
